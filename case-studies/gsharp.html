Gsharp Music Platform

Full-stack app for sharing, uploading, and streaming user-submitted music

Problem

Musicians needed a platform where they could share original tracks, comment, and connect with others in a secure and engaging way.

Solution

I developed a full-stack MERN application with authentication, protected routes, music upload/playback, and real-time interactions like likes and comments.

Result

The platform allowed artists to share their work in a supportive community space. Users could browse, engage, and interact securely, enhancing musical collaboration.

Technologies Used

  • React
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • MongoDB
  • JWT Auth
  • Render
