Why Do You Need a Website?

A website gives your business 24/7 visibility, builds trust, and allows for full control of your brand.

What’s the difference between Wix, Squarespace, and owning your own website?

Platforms like Wix/Squarespace are rentals. With custom-built sites, you own the code, hosting, and domain – total control, better SEO, and more flexibility.

How much should a website cost?

Pricing depends on features, but simple business sites can start at $500–$1500. Ongoing maintenance and hosting are additional.
