Web Design & Development Resources

Advice & answers to common client questions

💡 Why Do You Need a Website?

A website gives your business 24/7 visibility, builds trust, and lets you control your brand’s message across the internet.

🏠 Website Builders vs. Custom Websites

Platforms like Wix or Squarespace are like renting space — you don’t fully own the code. A custom website gives you total control, better performance, and full branding freedom.

💰 How Much Does a Website Cost?

Simple brochure-style sites typically range from $500 to $1500. E-commerce or advanced features increase the cost. Hosting and maintenance are usually billed monthly or yearly.

📈 What Makes a Website Successful?

Great websites are fast, mobile-friendly, and optimized for SEO. Clear calls to action and strong branding help convert visitors into customers.

🛠️ How Long Does It Take to Build a Website?

Basic websites take 1–3 weeks. Larger projects may take 1–2 months depending on content, revisions, and complexity.
